* To facilitate calculation, all calculated angles are default in radian.
* All input time should be in epoch UTC.

* sdipoledir.pro calculates dipole direction in GEO latitude and longitude. The angles are used in transform between GEO and MAG.

* sgmst.pro calculates Greenwich Mean Sidereal Time (GMST), this time converted to angle, is used in transform between GEI and GEO.

* sundir.pro calculates solar related angles. They are used in transform between GSE and GSM.
